Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

Data Collection

WordFlash is designed with your privacy as our top priority. We want to be completely transparent about what data we collect and how we use it.

Important: WordFlash does NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal data, browsing history, or learning records on remote servers.

The only data stored by WordFlash is:

  • Learning Progress: Your current learning level, mastered words, and study statistics
  • Word Library Configuration: Custom word lists, difficulty settings, and personal preferences
  • Extension Settings: Display frequency, notification preferences, and UI customization

All this data is stored exclusively in your browser's local storage using chrome.storage.local. This means your data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Data Usage

The data stored locally in your browser is used solely to provide and enhance your WordFlash learning experience:

  • To track your learning progress and adapt word difficulty
  • To remember your preferences and settings between browsing sessions
  • To personalize the word suggestions based on your learning history
  • To maintain consistency in your learning journey

No Data Sharing: We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any user data with third parties. Since all data remains on your device, there is no mechanism for data sharing.

WordFlash operates entirely offline in terms of data storage. The extension does not connect to any external servers for data processing or storage purposes.

Permissions

WordFlash requests the "host_permissions": [""] permission for a single, specific purpose:

Permission Purpose: To inject non-intrusive pop-up cards on web pages you visit, displaying English words for learning.

This permission is essential for WordFlash's core functionality but comes with important privacy safeguards:

  • No Tracking: We do not track your browsing behavior, visited URLs, or online activities
  • No Data Extraction: We do not read, collect, or transmit content from web pages you visit
  • No Behavioral Profiling: We do not create profiles based on your browsing habits
  • Limited Scope: The extension only adds visual elements (word cards) to pages; it does not interact with page content

The permission is a technical requirement of the Chrome Extensions platform to allow WordFlash to work across all websites. It does not grant us access to your personal data or browsing history.
